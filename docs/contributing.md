# Contributing to this Project

We love contributions! Whether you have a small correction or a massive new idea, here is how you can help:

## Ways to Participate
- **Reporting Issues:** Did you find something wrong? Open an [Issue](https://github.com/larisa-msoto/ai-bioinformatics-practice-model/issues) and let us know.
- **Proposing Edits:** Click the "Edit" pencil icon on any page. This will take you to GitHub's web editor. Once you save, you'll be creating a "Pull Request."
- **Community Discussions:** Join the conversation on our [Discussions page](https://github.com/larisa-msoto/ai-bioinformatics-practice-model/discussions).

## Our Contribution Process
1. **Fork the repo** (or just click edit in your browser).
2. **Make your changes** in a new branch.
3. **Submit a Pull Request** to the `main` branch.
4. **Wait for review.** One of our maintainers will check your changes for accuracy and adherence to our professional practice model.
5. **Get credited.** Once merged, we use the `all-contributors` bot to add your profile to our [contributors page](contributors.md)!

*Please note that this project is released with a [Code of Conduct](code_of_conduct.md). By participating, you agree to abide by its terms.*
