# 4D-Bio

Welcome to 4D-Bio! 

Inspired by Antrophic's 4D Framework for AI fluency, 4D-Bio extrapolates the 4 key competencies--Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence--into the professional practice of bioinformatics and computational biology. This repository serves as an open-source resource for the community to drive the integration of generative AI in bioinformatics. 

As trained experts, it is our responsibility to define the practical considerations for the responsible adoption of generative AI in the professional practice of bioinformatics. By defining how these technologies are adopted, we take ownership of our future, reclaim our agency, and advocate for our own value.

## Our Mission
To foster an inclusive and accountable professional community of bioinformaticians commited to upholding scientific rigor and ensuring reproducibility of computational life sciences research in the AI age.  

## Our purpose 
The main purpose of this repository is to capture discussions, resources, and lessons learned by international groups, amplifying the impact of regional efforts.

AI technology is moving faster than the rate of scientific publishing. The pace of new information is simply too overwhelming for any one of us to keep up with alone. By creating this repo, my hope is that anyone with something interesting to share can do so, regardless of their resources.

We are all concerned about the same challenges. Let’s work on them together in a softly organized way. No one should have to repeat work that others have already done. Through collaboration, the effort of one person is multiplied by the force of the community around them.

This is our profession, and we need to treat it as such. We cannot let big-AI decide how bioinformatics gets done—the consequences are too enormous.

AI should not replace bioinformaticians. It is our responsibility to make it clear to decision-makers why we are a critical piece of life sciences research. We must position bioinformaticians as the angular stone of all multimillion-dollar investments in genomics projects. No one can do this alone, we must do it together. 

## How to use this site
This site is a living document. You can navigate through the practice model using the sidebar. If you have questions or ideas, we invite you to participate in our community discussions at the bottom of each page or contribute directly to this text by clicking the edit button at the top right.
