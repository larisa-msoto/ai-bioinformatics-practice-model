# Diligence

!!! note ""
    According to [Anthropic's 4D Framework for AI fluency](https://www-cdn.anthropic.com/334975cdec18f744b4fa511dc8518bd8d119d29d.pdf?ref=hackshackers.com) Diligence means _"Taking responsibility for what we do with AI and how we use it."_.

In Bioinformatics, diligence is assuming accountability and responsibility for the impact of agentic AI solutions. This is not only about adhering to international standards for ethical and safe AI use, but also about maintaining the cognitive abilities to remain critical of AI-generated outputs and complete tasks manually when AI agents fail.

## Ensuring pipeline governance and regulatory compliance

* Professional Conduct (ISCB Competency Framework)
    * [ISCB J3: Comply with professional, ethical, legal, and social standards.](https://competency.ebi.ac.uk/framework/iscb/3.0/competency/details/7946)

* AI regulatory alignment
    * Monitor rapidly shifting regional and [global AI legal frameworks](https://github.com/melissawm/open-source-ai-contribution-policies) to dynamically align pipeline deployments with local jurisdiction mandates.

* Ethical evaluation
    * Identifying algorithmic bias and ensuring that AI-driven decisions are calibrated, safe, and legally compliant.
    * Formulate institutional/team risk guardrails to prevent algorithmic discrimination, dual-use biological research hazards, or intellectual property infringements.

# Guiding institutional strategy and cross-sector diplomacy

* Visionary stewardship
    * Design long-term technical roadmaps, governance models, and sustainability plans to secure the permanent operational longevity of bioinformatics initiatives.

* Scientific diplomacy
    * Navigate complex negotiations and communicate impact across academia, industry, and government sectors to align competing interests and influence decision makers.

## Elevating workforce capacity and educational legacy

* Generational mentorship
    * Develop early-career talent by actively mentoring them to leverage generative AI systems appropriately, critically, and responsibly.

* Pedagogical dissemination
    * Design open-access learning resources that leverage cloud and AI technologies to democratize access to scientific discoveries and dismantle structural educational inequities.
